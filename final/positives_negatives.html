Positives and Negatives

Positives

A Positive

There are plenty of positives with having a child with down syndrome along with a few cons too. One of the positives is that you yourself will grow so much as a person. You will gain patience, become more compassionate, start enjoying the small simple things, and become a advocate for those in need. When you have a down syndrome child you start to become more humble and kind do others, and as a result you will become more happier.

A Positive

Down syndrome kids tend to not have an ego, and because of that they are more compassionate and have more empathy. Down syndrome kids go through anger emotions and all that stuff but when they get back to being calm they forget what they were upset with and forgive you or whoever almost right away.

Negatives

A Negative

A negative about having a down syndrome child is the fact that they may have a lot of health problems. Some of these are respiratory infections, heart defects, thyroid disorders, etc. Parents may have to do a lot of surgeries and check ups and procedures. That isn't the harderest part though, the hardest part is seeing the hardships your child is going through and dealing with that heartache. But you can get through it, it'll be worth it.

A Negative

Another negative would be that it can cause anxiety on the parents in social situations. Your child may have breakdowns in public and others accidents may happen as well. You may feel that others are judging you and that they may not treat you well. But again as with the other negative above, it's all worth it and you'll be able to handle it.
